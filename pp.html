Privacy Policy

BA ADVANCED Discord Bot

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how BA ADVANCED (the "Bot") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use it on Discord. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

1. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and store the following data to function properly:

Discord Server Information

Server ID, channel IDs, role IDs, and server settings configured for the Bot.

Discord User Information

User IDs, usernames, discriminators, and avatars (when necessary for specific commands like profile systems).

User-Provided Content

Data you explicitly provide through commands (e.g., configuration settings, custom messages, reminders, tags).

Usage Data

Command usage logs (which command was run, by which user, in which server, and at what time) for debugging, analytics, and abuse prevention.

Note: We do not collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, email addresses, or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the core features of the Bot.
  • To personalize user experience (e.g., server-specific configurations).
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse.
  • For internal analytics to improve the Bot's performance and features.
  • To comply with legal obligations when required.

3. Data Storage and Security

Storage

Data is stored securely on servers provided by our hosting provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Retention

  • Server configuration data is retained until a server removes the Bot.
  • User-specific data (like reminder texts) is deleted when the relevant function is completed or upon user/server request.
  • Usage logs may be kept for a short period (typically 30 days) for operational and security purposes.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

Data may be disclosed only in the following limited circumstances:

  • To comply with a legal obligation or lawful request from authorities.
  • To protect and defend the rights or property of the Developer.
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Bot.
  • To protect the personal safety of users of the Bot or the public.

5. User Rights (Your Choices)

You have the right to:

  • Opt-Out: Stop using the Bot. You can remove it from your server at any time.
  • Data Access & Deletion: Request to view or delete your user-specific data. To do so, please contact us via the main documentation page. Server administrators can control server-specific data via Bot commands.
  • Disable Data Collection: The only way to fully disable data collection is to stop interacting with and remove the Bot from your server.

6. Third-Party Services

The Bot may link to or use third-party services (e.g., external APIs for weather, music, or other functionalities). Their privacy policies govern your interaction with those services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personal information from Children. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided data, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Bot after changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
